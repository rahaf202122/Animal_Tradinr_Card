Snake

A calm and non-venomous snake known for curling into a tight ball when it feels threatened.

  • Scientific Name: Python regius
  • Average Length: 90–150 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 20–30 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands and forests of West and Central Africa

Ball pythons are popular pet snakes because of their gentle temperament and manageable size. They are non-venomous constrictors, meaning they subdue their prey by wrapping around it. Their unique patterns and vibrant colors make them one of the most recognizable snake species in the reptile world.
